\u2018If Divya Bharti was alive today, a lot of people would be out of work\u2019: Tridev actor Sonam Khan remembers her only friend in the film industry<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Yesteryear actor Sonam Khan, who is best known for her popular song from the film Tridev, has opened up about her friendship with the late actor Divya Bharti<\/strong>. Sonam said that Divya was a legend, and added that there was never any competition between them. The actor also recalled a hilarious anecdote about how they were once comparing their waist sizes. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

In an interview with Siddharth Kannan, Sonam said, \u201cIt worked between us (her and Divya) because we were the same age, we laughed with each other and there was no competition between us. She even knew that I was going to quit acting. She was very nice. If she was alive today, a lot of people would be out of work.\u201d<\/p>\n\n\n\n

She added, \u201cShe was a very happy-go-lucky person and you get to see very few people like her. And the whole world knows that she was magic in front of the camera. Her looks were amazing and she was also a very bold girl, very ahead of her times. She would answer back to people on their face, be it any matter.\u201d<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
